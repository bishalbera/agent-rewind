# Shipping and delivery

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express shipping takes 1–2 days.

- Use `get_shipping_status` to fetch the live carrier status for an order.
- "processing" means the order has not shipped yet — there is no tracking
  number until it ships.
- If a package is marked delivered but the customer has not received it, ask
  them to check with neighbours and building reception before opening a claim.
